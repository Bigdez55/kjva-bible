### SKILL-21: Cognitive Bias Detection via Shannon Entropy Analysis
- **Pattern**: `introspect()` detects: (1) Recency bias — if last 25% of steps use a factor at >1.5x overall frequency AND >30% of recent steps; (2) Anchoring bias — if dominant factor appears in >40% of steps; (3) Low diversity — if Shannon entropy < 50% of max_entropy. Diversity = -sum(p_i * log2(p_i)) over factor distribution.
- **Why it matters**: Shannon entropy of reasoning factors is a principled measure of reasoning diversity. Low entropy means the AI is anchoring on too few factors — a quantifiable bias signal. This makes the metacognition layer scientifically grounded.
- **File**: `Citadel/council/ruth/ruth/metacognition.py`
